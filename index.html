Welcome to MatchUP

MatchUP is a new app that helps you to find your perfect match for your favorite sport.

Let's say you like playing tennis. Yet you do not have a partner, maybe because you just recently moved to a new city and do not know many people there yet. Or maybe you just want to find some new partners to play with in order to improve your game.

So how do you find persons to play with? You might just check out some tennis clubs and talk to people, but what are the chances that you find someone that matches your skill level and is a likeable person? Not really high, right!?

So go ahead and check out how MatchUP can help with situations like these!

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for everyone to have fun at their preferred sport. Therefore we aim to connect people who live in the same area and are interested in similar sports. Additionally for some sports (like tennis) it make sense to know the skill level of your partners. So we added a way to rate and understand your partners proficiency in order for you to find better matches.
